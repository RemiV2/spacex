We can see a launching base also called a spaceport, it is a set of installations intended to allow the preparation, the launching and the follow-up of the first phases of flight of a launcher. It includes one or more launch sets, logistical facilities and facilities for the implementation of safeguarding measures. Assembly buildings in which the launcher and its payload (artificial satellite, space probe, ...) are assembled; a payload preparation building ;one or more launching areas (or launching areas) from which the rockets are fired. The presence of several launch pads can shorten the launch time.
