About SpaceX

Our site is an immersive site for the user to imagine the project of SpaceX.
SpaceX is a manufacturer specializing in aerospace, designing Falcon launchers, Merlin and Raptor engines, and the Dragon cargo ship that supplies the International Space Station (ISS). Based in Hawthorne (California, USA), SpaceX was founded in 2002 by Elon Musk who is also the leader.
The US company SpaceX announces that two tourists will make a private flight around the Moon by the end of 2018. Travelers will go where the Man has not ventured for 50 years, ie since the Apollo mission 17 of NASA, in December 1972.
